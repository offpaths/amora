## Design Context

### Users

Amora's website is primarily for single men aged roughly 24–38 who arrive from dating-advice creators and other referral platforms. They are checking whether Amora can help them plan a thoughtful, personal date without spending hours researching.

### Brand Personality

Soft, romantic, and elegant. Amora should feel premium and intentional: a quiet source of confidence, never manipulative or overpromising.

### Aesthetic Direction

Warm ivory paper, oxblood, brass, and olive create a refined editorial feel. The website is light because people encounter it casually through social and creator recommendations, often on a phone. Motion should be restrained, calm, and purposeful.

### Design Principles

- Make personalization—not generic date ideas—the central value.
- Lead with considered confidence and emotional warmth.
- Keep the initial experience fast, legible, and easy to trust.
- Use one restrained, orchestrated entry moment rather than decorative motion throughout.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences completely.
