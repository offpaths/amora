A better plan than dinner and guessing.

Amora turns lightweight context into a date that feels specific to this person, this week, and this mood.

1. Tell us about her

Share what she likes, what would make the date feel personal, the area you want to plan near, and the kind of night you want to create.

2. Get a thoughtful plan

Amora builds a 3-stop plan around her interests, your budget, the mood, and places that make sense together nearby.

3. Arrive with intention

Show up with a plan that feels considered, specific, and easy to follow, instead of asking what she wants to do at the last minute.

Premium by design.

Amora is not an infinite date-ideas library. It is a planning assistant for someone who wants the date to feel intentional without spending hours researching.

What the app plans around

  • What would make this feel personal.
  • A nearby planning area, not raw coordinates.
  • Budget comfort for the full date for two people.
  • Vibe, duration, and no-drinking preferences.
  • Three stops that stay close enough for a short walk or rideshare.

Clear about AI and privacy.

Amora explains what is sent for generation before the intake form and does not collect in-app analytics.

  • Planning area, preferences, and personal context are sent to the AI provider to generate a date plan.
  • Location detection is optional; users can type a planning area instead.
  • Amora uses the planning area you provide, not raw latitude or longitude.
  • App performance, purchase, and subscription reporting come from App Store Connect.
  • Amora does not sell personal information.
