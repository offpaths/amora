Support

For help with Amora, contact support@planwithamora.com.

Include this information

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • Any error message shown in the app.
  • Whether the issue is about plan generation, location, purchase, restore, or subscription management.

Purchases

Purchases are handled by Apple. You can manage subscriptions in App Store account settings. Refund requests are handled through Apple's purchase support flow.
