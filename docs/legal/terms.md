# Amora Terms of Use Draft

Publish this content at https://planwithamora.com/terms before App Review.

Amora provides AI-assisted date-plan previews and paid unlocked itineraries. Generated plans are suggestions only. Users are responsible for checking venue availability, hours, prices, safety, accessibility, transportation, and suitability before visiting.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit:

- Amora Plus Monthly unlocks unlimited thoughtful date plans while the subscription is active.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Payment is charged to your Apple Account. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in App Store account settings.

Use of Amora is also subject to Apple's Standard End User License Agreement unless a custom EULA is published in App Store Connect.
